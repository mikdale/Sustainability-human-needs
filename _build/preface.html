Sustainability and Human Needs

This book is motivated by my teaching of an Environmental Sustainability class. I was constantly looking for ways to embed the environmental and sustainability issues in the everyday experience of the students' lives.

The evidence is fairly clear that human activity is having a dramatic impact on the natural environment. We are changing the climate on an unprecendet scale, species are becoming extinct at apocalyptic rates, we are alse disrupting many other natural cycles.
